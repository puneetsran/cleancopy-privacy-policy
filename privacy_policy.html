CleanCopy Privacy Policy

CleanCopy does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

All processing—including table extraction, cleaning, and CSV export—happens entirely on the user’s device.

The extension only accesses webpage content when the user activates it. No data is sent to external servers.

CleanCopy does not track browsing history, usage activity, or any personally identifiable information.
